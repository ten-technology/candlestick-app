Introduction

Welcome to Candlestick Game! This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is collected, used, and protected when using our app. At Ten Technology Solutions (referred to as "we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is very important to us.

By using the Candlestick Game app (the "App"), you consent to the terms set forth in this Privacy Policy. Please read this document carefully to understand your rights and obligations. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, do not use the App.

1. Information the App Collects

a. User-Provided Information

The App allows users to input and manage:

  • Account credentials (username, email)
  • Trading preferences and strategy selections
  • Virtual trading activity and portfolio data

This information is used to personalize your experience and track your virtual trading performance. User trading data is stored securely on our servers.

b. Automatically Collected Data

We may collect non-personal data automatically to improve the App's performance:

  • App usage information: Crash reports, feature usage statistics, and error logs via Firebase.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system version, and language settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

User account data is used to:

  • Maintain your trading account and portfolio
  • Display personalized trading history and performance metrics
  • Populate leaderboards and competitive rankings
  • Enable cross-device synchronization of your progress

Anonymized app usage data may be used for:

  • Troubleshooting technical issues
  • Improving app features and user experience
  • Analytics and performance optimization

We do not sell your personal data to third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All account information is encrypted during transmission and storage. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
