Introduction

Welcome to Candlestick Game! This Privacy Policy outlines how data is handled when using our app. At Ten Technology Solutions (referred to as "we," "us," or "our"), your privacy is very important to us.

By using the Candlestick Game app (the "App"), you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, do not use the App.

1. What We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from you.

Candlestick Game does not require you to create an account, provide your name, email, or any other personal data. Your gameplay experience is completely private and stored locally on your device.

All game progress, trades, and portfolio data are saved only on your device. We do not access, store, or transmit this information to our servers.

2. Apple App Store Data

When you download and use Candlestick Game from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect certain information directly. This includes:

  • Device identifiers and technical information
  • App store analytics and download information
  • Usage data for app performance monitoring

This data collection is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We have no control over what Apple collects or how they use it. For more information, please review Apple's Privacy Policy.

3. Third-Party Services

Candlestick Game does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services. We do not track your activity, behavior, or performance within the app.

4. Data Security

Since we don't collect any personal information, there is minimal privacy risk. Your game data remains stored locally on your device with the security protections provided by your operating system.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
